Immerse Yourself in Style



FashionTek is the creator of Boutique, a luxurious 3D virtual dressing room which helps online shoppers make the right purchase, the first time around. Users create an accurate 3D model of themselves to try-on photorealistic clothing, mixing and matching their favorite items, to ensure correct fit and style. To enhance the experience, users can view their creations in motion, 360-degrees or full-sized through augmented reality (AR). Best of all, Boutique seamlessly integrates with e-commerce retailers.​​
